Chill Out With The Barber


This walk departs from the Greymouth Town Clock at 8:00am. Pre-booking is essential to guarantee that a guide will be available. Begin by walking out onto the viewing platform into the centre of the Barber, and then a walk along the floodwall to gain views of the Barber as it escapes out to sea. Doubling back past the courthouse and Guiness street for some more local history. Total time 60 minutes including the return to the Town Clock.

Be aware that temperatures inside the Barber can reach below zero, and the wind can be forceful and cold. Please dress appropriately for the expected conditions.

All tours include commentary by professional storytellers.

We cannot guarantee an enhanced katabatic wind on any given day, but the Barber runs regularly from April – November each year, and periodically outside these months. On most days you will certainly feel the wind if not experiencing the fog and mist.
