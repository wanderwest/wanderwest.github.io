Storytelling Performances for Schools

Early Years – Stories for Busy Bodies
"Stories you can move to, for bodies that can’t sit still!"
A high-energy, hands-on session packed with movement, actions, and participation. Perfect for young children building confidence through joyful storytelling.

Primary – Tales from the Edge of the World
"Adventures to the edges of New Zealand…and the imagination!"
Explore stories from New Zealand’s remote landscapes and rich traditions, alongside global folktales. Sparks curiosity and inspires creative thinking.

Teenage – Stories to Speak Of
"Find your voice. Tell your story. Speak your world."
Dive into traditional tales, historical chronicles, and personal narratives. Gain confidence in storytelling using the 5 languages of storytelling.

These are brief summations of storytelling performances we can work with individual schools to build programmes that are tailored to their needs - including workshops for most age levels.
